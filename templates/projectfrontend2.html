WHY PREDICT?

Now-a-days, animals face various diseases due to the environmental condition and their living habits. Like Humans, Animal can not convey how they are feeling. Accurate and on-time analysis of any health-related problem is important for the prevention and treatment of the illness. That’s how we got the inspiration to do for our project. Which could be a great solution for this problem .Our project will help people to get to know about what kind of disease animal has at an early stage so they can help their pets or other animal .
